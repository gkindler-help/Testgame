THE HOME BATTLE: BUYER BEWARE

This is a fresh run-and-gun build inspired by arcade side-scrollers. Fight your way through a chaotic housing market. George is the required power-up. Get him, or the deal dies at the boss.

Enemy Types

  • Lipstick Flip — fast ground attacker
  • Overpriced Listing — armored slow mover
  • Appraisal Drone — flying ranged enemy
  • Wire Fraud Orb — dangerous projectile barrage

Win Condition

Reach the final boss and destroy it. George activates late in the mission and unlocks the only weapon mode that can actually crack the boss armor in time.
