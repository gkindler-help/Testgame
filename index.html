How to play

  • Move: Arrow keys or A / D
  • Jump: Space, W, or ↑
  • Reach the sign at the end of each level
  • Collect coins and power-ups
  • Avoid hazards like bad flips, wire fraud, and inspection traps

Worlds

  1. Pre-Approval Plains
  2. Showing Sprint
  3. Inspection Dungeon

Ready to buy without getting wrecked?

Collect smart choices. Avoid real-estate traps. Reach the closing sign with enough budget and low enough stress to survive the deal.

What items mean

Knowledge coin = smart move
Power-up = budget boost / stress drop
Hazard = bad decision or hidden issue
Finish sign = close the deal

Theme

This MVP is built so you can expand it into a bigger buyer journey later: more levels, local jokes, side quests, lender NPCs, inspection mini-games, and neighborhood-specific worlds.
